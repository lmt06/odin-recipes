Cheesy Baked Green Beans

Description

A family favorite thanksgiving dish that basically only requires opeing a bag of green beans dumping on cheese then putting in the oven. Comes out of the oven tasting like it took three hours of prep! Perfect for the slacker cook this holiday season!

Yields: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 lb. green beans, cleaned with ends removed
  • 1/2 c. heavy cream
  • 2 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 2 tsp. lemon zest
  • 1 c. mozzarella
  • 2/3 c. freshly grated Parmesan, plus more for garnish
  • pinch of salt
