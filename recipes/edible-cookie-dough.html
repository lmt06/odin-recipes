Edible Cookie Dough

Description

A favorite snack of mine that's totally unhealthy but definitely satisfying

Yield: 8 servings

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
Steps:
  1. microwave butter for about 10 seconds to thaw
  2. Combine all ingredients in mixing bowl
  3. Stir, pour in chocolate chips and enjoy
