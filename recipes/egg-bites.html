Cheesy Bacon Egg Bites


Description:

A more healthy way to start your day recommened my my physical trainer, May.
Once made a delicious, easier, and healthier breakfast

Yields: 24 egg bites

Ingredients:
  • 7 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 and 1/2 cups shredded monterey jack & cheddar cheese, divided
  • 3/4 cup real cooked bacon bits
